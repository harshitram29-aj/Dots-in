The problem with current AI

AI knows what you do. It has no idea who you are.

ChatGPT predicts your next word. Netflix predicts your next watch. But neither understands why you made the choices you did — your mood that day, your cognitive style, your biological rhythms. That gap is what Dots-In is closing.

How it works

From human data to human understanding.

The LBM doesn't just read what you do. It builds a living model of who you are — and updates in real time as you change.

01
Collect across sources
Wearables, apps like Apple Health and Google Fit, environmental sensors, and biological markers. Data from the body, not just the browser. DNA stays local — edge-computed, encrypted.
02
Build your behavioural profile
The LBM integrates genomics, neuropsychology, cognitive patterns, and real-time physiological data into a single unified profile — a digital twin that's as unique as your DNA.
03
Adapt in real time
Unlike a static model, the LBM evolves as you do. Your stress levels today change what it recommends. Your sleep last night changes how it talks to you. AI that moves with human life.
04
Power any application
The LBM is a horizontal infrastructure layer. Any app, platform, or device can connect to it via API — and immediately gain deep human understanding without building it from scratch.
